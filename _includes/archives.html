Since 2000, RMLL (Rencontres Mondiales du Logiciel Libre a.k.a. Libre Software Meeting) is an open access, free as in free beer, annual conference about Free Software, hosted in a different town each year. It hosts multiple tracks (Education, Economy, Technical ...) about Free Software in order to set up bridges between communities.

In 2016, there is no global RMLL because no local Free Software User group volunteered.
But, we, the Security Track chairmen, have decided to organize nevertheless our track because ... we love to bring to you brillant speakers on Free Software and Security.

Just have a look on the previous editions and the speakers who came to speak at RMLL Security Track.
